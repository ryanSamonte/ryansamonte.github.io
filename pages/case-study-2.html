In the Palm of Your Hand: A Value-Driven Educational App

Designed a mobile app that will match the use case of students and parents on the current website and at the same time generate another channel of revenue for B2B partners

Duration:

January 31- February 12, 2022
(2 weeks)

Contribution:

User Research, Wireframing and Prototyping

Team:

Marga Hernandez, Jayne Tinhay Arriola, Kathrina Tacastacas

Tools Used:

Notion, Whimsical, Figma

Challenges

  The need to explore the mobile platform as majority of the users are accessing the current website through their mobile devices


  B2B partners are keen on reaching out the users through a mobile app

Solution

  Explore current use cases on the website and find ways to integrate it into the mobile app design


  Improve the mobile app design so it can also cater the needs of the parent users

First question that we asked ourselves is: How might we design a user-friendly, accessible, and value-driven mobile app that connects and meets the needs of students, parents, and B2B partners of Edukasyon.ph?

Research

To answer the question on our mind, we conducted a user interview with 14 participants (7 students, 4 parents, 2 B2B partners, and 1 Edukasyon.ph team member). With those participants, we came up with 3 student personas and 1 persona. Letâ€™s meet each of them:

Clarence, Incoming College student, 18

Use Case:

Edukasyon is used to find the best schools and programs for students.

Pain Point:

Having to call schools one by one for information due to outdated or limited information online.

"Edukasyon is the sole website for information regarding college applications, schools, and courses...this is where I found my course and the best schools for my course."

Emily, Graduating College Student, 22

Use Case:

Edukasyon enables users to explore careers relevant to their course, view career tips and advice, and choose from a wide selection of jobs and internships.

Pain Point:

Limited resources and opportunities for finding internships relevant to her course and career path.

"Edukasyon helped me in finding and applying for an internship through their articles and tips on applying, making a resume, and acing the interview."

Lily, College Student, 17

Use Case:

Edukasyon allows users to learn and get inspiration from scholars, alumni, professionals, and industry leaders to solidify their academic and career paths.

Pain Point:

Unsure about her course and the options she can explore given her current skills and program.

"I visit Edukasyon every time I find it difficult to dream...I was really struggling in my career path...and Edukasyon presented many options to me."

Edgar, Chill Parent, 52

Use Case:

Edukasyon allows parents to view relevant information about the schools and courses their children are interested in.

Pain Point:

Having to look for information about schools and courses one by one, whether through call or school websites.

"[Edukasyon is] for students and parents to have an informed and educated decision when it comes to choosing schools and courses."

Solution

Previous Next

Highlight the main information that users always look for in schools and courses.

Users can see the important details they are looking like tuition fee, course duration, number of programs offered, accreditation and location

Previous Next

Create an easy way to compare options with the help of a side-by-side comparison

Users can now easily compare school or courses side-by-side, to help them in making informed decision

Previous Next

Allow users to locate schools or universities that are near their home or location

Users can have an option to search for school or course that is near to their home or current location

Testing the Design

To validate our solution we conducted a usability testing with 9 participants (5 students and 4 parents) and here are the feedback we got:

Users find it convenient and most important that they can compare schools side-by-side without needing excel sheets.

Users found the app easy to navigate and likes the layout

Users like that you can now quickly see and absorb relevant information
